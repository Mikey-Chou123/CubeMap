3x3 Roadmap

What is the 3x3?

The 3x3x3 Rubik's Cube is the classic puzzle cube with 6 faces and 54 colored stickers. It's the most popular speedcubing event and the foundation for learning other twisty puzzles.

Follow your speedcubing path.

Classic 7-step beginner approach. Focuses on understanding the cube structure and solving layer by layer.

Basic Block Building

Learn 1x2x3 block building and basic M-slice moves while minimizing rotations.

  • Focus: First two blocks & basic M-moves
