3x3

3x3 Rubik’s Cube — the world’s best-selling puzzle with over 43 quintillion possible scrambles. It’s the face of the World Cube Association (WCA) and the ultimate test of speed, skill, and style in cubing.

CFOP

The most famous and widely used method for solving the 3x3. Built around 4 main steps:
Cross, F2L, OLL, PLL

Roux

A block-building method that emphasizes efficiency, fewer moves, and a unique approach to solving.
