LBL Method (Layer By Layer)

The Layer-by-Layer (LBL) method is the most popular beginner approach to solving the Rubik's Cube. You solve the cube one layer at a time ‚Äî first the cross, then the first two layers, and finally the last layer. It's simple, intuitive, and a great foundation before moving on to advanced methods like CFOP.

The LBL method teaches a simple, step-by-step system:

  • Step 1: Solve the White Cross (The Daisy).
  • Step 2: Complete the First Layer Corners.
  • Step 3: Solve the Second Layer Edges.
  • Step 4: Form the Last Layer Cross (Orient Edges).
  • Step 5: Position the Last Layer Corners (Permute Corners).
  • Step 6: Twist the Last Layer Corners (Orient Corners).
  • Step 7: Position the Last Layer Edges (Permute Edges).
