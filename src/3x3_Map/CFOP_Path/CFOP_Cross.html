CFOP Method: Cross

The first step of CFOP, the Cross, sets the foundation for the solve.

Learn more about the Cross
  • Usually solvable in 8 moves or fewer.
  • Always solve it on the bottom layer.
  • Planning and alignment here makes the rest of CFOP faster.

Key Tips for Cross

  • Plan your cross on the bottom layer for faster solves.
  • Try to complete the cross in fewer moves.
  • Look ahead while solving to reduce pause time.
  • Practice aligning edges before making the first move.
